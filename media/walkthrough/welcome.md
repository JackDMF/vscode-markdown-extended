## Markdown Extended Pro

Extra Markdown syntax in the built-in preview, plus a what‑you‑see‑is‑what‑you‑get exporter to **HTML, PDF, PNG, and JPG**.

- **Rich syntax** — admonitions, containers, sidenotes, footnotes, sub/superscript, marks, abbreviations, inline attributes, and a table of contents.
- **Self‑contained exports** — styles and images are embedded into a single file.
- **Mermaid diagrams** are rendered into exports automatically.
